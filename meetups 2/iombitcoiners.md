The Isle of Man is fast becoming a shining light of the Bitcoin renaissance and I’m a pretty sure renaissance 2.0 will be much more fun when shared with friends.

With a growing list of over 30 merchants accepting bitcoin, it’s a great time to visit (and a bit closer than El Salvador).

So, if you’re on the island or planning a visit, your fellow bitcoiners are waiting in their [Telegram](http://t.me/IOMBitcoiners) channel or, you can contact [Molly](http://twitter.com/coincornermolly) on Twitter for more details.
