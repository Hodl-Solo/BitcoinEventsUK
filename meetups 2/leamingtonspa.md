If you’re in the Leamington Spa area get in touch with this thriving Bitcoin community, originally started by [Orange Surf](http://twitter.com/orangesurfbtc).

It’s a great central location for a meetup full of knowledgable bitcoiners.

Meetups will be monthly and held at The Star & Garter, CV32 5LL.

Join them on the last Wednesday of the month for a casual chat and drinks at 19:00. Beginners welcome.

For all the details join them on [Telegram](http://t.me/leambitcoin) and follow on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/leambitcoin).
