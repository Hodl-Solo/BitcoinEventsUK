Bitcoin Power is a new social gathering in Ayr, Scotland, bringing people together to explore Bitcoin and the importance of financial sovereignty.

Over the coming months, the group will cover topics such as privacy, security, wallets, KYC, running nodes, and more. The first few sessions will focus on introductions and finding out what direction members would like discussions to take.

The meetup is open to everyone, from complete beginners to long-time Bitcoiners. Business owners curious about accepting Bitcoin payments are especially welcome.

Bitcoin Power isn’t just about connecting with like-minded individuals – it’s also about sharing practical ways to protect and grow wealth in today’s economy.
