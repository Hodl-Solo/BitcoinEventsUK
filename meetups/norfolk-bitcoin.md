Norfolk Bitcoin is dedicated to promoting Bitcoin adoption in the Norfolk area. They provide a space for local individuals and businesses who want to learn about and use Bitcoin. Join their regular meetups to network, chat, and exchange ideas with other enthusiasts.  
  
For details of their upcoming events you can find and follow them on Twitter and Instagram.

Twitter: <https://twitter.com/norfolkbitcoin>

Instagram: <https://instagram.com/norfolkbitcoin>

You’ll also find some of the members in the Suffolk [Telegram](https://linktr.ee/suffolkbitcoin) group.
