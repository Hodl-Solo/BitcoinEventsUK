Are you interested in Bitcoin and live in Devon? Then you’re in luck! The Devon Bitcoiners meet on an ad-hoc basis in the amazing county of Devon. This is a great opportunity to meet other Bitcoin enthusiasts and learn more about the future of money. The group is very welcoming and has a host of members ready to help all newcomers. Whether you’re a seasoned Bitcoiner or just starting out, this is the perfect place to learn more and connect with like-minded individuals.

Many of the members also attend the nearby, regular meetups at [Plymouth Bitcoiners](https://bitcoinevents.uk/plymouthbitcoiners/).
